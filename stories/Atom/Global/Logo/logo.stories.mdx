# UNDP Logos
The UNDP logo is available in two versions: blue, and white. Each has its own purpose and should be used appropriately, as per official branding guidelines, depending on available screen space and target audience.

<ul>
<li>Logos are generally used in the header or footer components</li>
<li>For large devices, the dimensions are (width:60px, Height:122px) and for small devices the dimensions will be converted to (width:60px, Height:70px)</li>
</ul>
